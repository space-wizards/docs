## Overview

The Zombies game mode will now have an emphasis on zombies trying to make it to Central Command; a CBURN squad will thus be dispatched automatically to protect Evac when zombies begin overtaking the station. As often happens in zombie survival movies and games, the military (CBURN) will defend some vital installation (Evac) and the round should climax in a fight between zombies and the crew (aided by CBURN) to secure Evac.

To put emphasis on the zombie apocalypse survival vibe of the game mode, ambuzol + will be made CBURN / admeme exclusive, and thus the zombie virus will no longer be completely curable.

On the other hand, crew will now be able to be way more resistant to the infection trough protective clothing (let’s put those bio suits to use!)

Finally, to aid in Zombies seeing overtaking Central Command as their goal, they will receive new victory conditions revolving around infecting CentComm.

## Background

Currently, the Zombies game mode usually goes like this: 
 - Initial infected crew members prepare a bit for the outbreak.
 - Outbreak happens.
 - Zombies are either contained due to fast ambuzol+ production or completely overrun the station and have an evacuation be forced on the crew by central command.

The second outcome end up with plenty of bored ghost players (since zombies are always butchered by the security force, even when they overtake the station). This isn’t good.

When zombies overrun the shift, the average player’s experience will be just wandering aimlessly as a zombie trying to bite one or two survivors. These survivors will, however, enjoy the round *a lot*. It is very fun to make a last stand against the horde. Sadly, when only two persons out of 80 actually enjoy a shift for its most part, things need to change.

CBURN should make that "last stand" vibe available to more crew members.

The removal of ambuzol+ production will also make it so Zombies can no longer just be wiped out and ignored for the rest of the shift: crew members will now have to go to Medway when a lone surviving zombie bites them instead of just shrugging it off due to immunity.

## Features to be added

CBURN, currently an admeme-exclusive, will now spawn when Evac is called by the Zombies game mode. They will have one main goal: prevent any infected individual from reaching CentComm.

Zombies will also need a rework in their objectives to make it more clear they want to infect the station, then Central Command. These objectives will also give something for CBURN to aim to counter.

## Game Design Rationale

This should enhance the lackluster end of the Zombies game mode, and provide something fun for ghost players to yearn for.

A proper win condition for the Initial Infecteds & friends will also make for a clearer goal to strive for (currently, Zombies lack a proper greentext.)

Removing ambuzol+ will ensure even a failed Zombies round will still make wandering alone scary (there could be remaining zombies!)

## Roundflow & Player interaction

CBURN players shouldn’t be enforced to save crew members; the players will probably do it anyway, but the idea is for Quarantine Officers to just keep Evac safe until it leaves free of any infected personnel, since Central Command is selfish. Crew members will thus have to prove their absence of any infection to CBURN so that they may get on the shuttle, all while defending it from a horde of zombies.

Removing the permanent zombie protection from ambuzol+ will also amount to more interaction with Medbay, and introduce potential role-play situations.
